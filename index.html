About Us

Up until 1939 strawberries were imported from Britian and Holland into Ireland. As a result of World War Two Ireland had to cultivate its own strawberries. Wexford, known as the "Sunny South East" of Ireland, Became the perfect location to grow. Wexford strawberries have become so popular that there is even a festival dedicated to the fruit. Now you can have these delicious strawberries delivered right to your door!
